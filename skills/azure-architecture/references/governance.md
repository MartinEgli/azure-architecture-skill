# Azure Governance Reference

Use for `/azure governance`.

## Governance Areas

- Management group and subscription ownership
- Azure Policy and initiative structure
- RBAC and privileged access
- Naming, tagging, and resource organization
- Environment separation and lifecycle controls
- IaC standards and deployment gates
- Monitoring, logging, alert routing, and retention
- Cost management ownership and budgets
- Security exceptions, policy exemptions, and review cadence

## Output Expectations

- Define guardrails, owners, enforcement point, exception path, and evidence.
- Identify what can be automated by policy or IaC.
- Hand precise role assignment generation to `azure-rbac`.
- Hand compliance/security audit execution to `azure-compliance`.
